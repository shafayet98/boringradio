About 5:30 AM

5:30am is for those who are going through some shit. An unforgiving hour. It's for long-haul commuters and tech bros with their cold plunges, for marathon runners and sleepwalkers, insomniacs and parents of newborns, manual labours and shop openners. I am none of these things, my deadlines are self-imposed and I'm not really into life optimization. And all the other hours of the day are fine with their variations in light and sound and scent. But I've found 5:30am to be the best hour because that's when the world finally shuts up and gives you just enough light to find what you're looking for.
